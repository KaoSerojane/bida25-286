Our Legal Services

At KP and Associates, we provide a range of legal services designed to support both individuals and businesses across Botswana. Our key areas of expertise include.

1. Corporate Law We assist businesses with legal matters related to their formation, operation, and compliance. This includes company registration, drafting shareholder agreements, ensuring regulatory compliance, and advising on corporate governance. Our goal is to help businesses operate smoothly while minimizing legal risks.

2. Family Law We provide legal support in sensitive family matters such as divorce, child custody, maintenance, and guardianship. Our approach is compassionate and professional, ensuring that clients receive fair and practical solutions during difficult times.

3. Property Law Our firm offers guidance on property transactions, including buying, selling, and leasing property. We also assist with title deeds, property disputes, and ensuring that all legal requirements are met in real estate dealings.

4. Contract Law We help clients draft, review, and enforce contracts to ensure their rights are protected. Whether it is business agreements or personal contracts, we make sure the terms are clear, fair, and legally binding.

5. Civil Litigation We represent clients in non-criminal disputes in court, including matters involving contracts, property, and other civil issues. Our team works to resolve disputes efficiently, whether through negotiation, mediation, or litigation.
