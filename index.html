KP & Associates is a professional law firm based in Botswana, dedicated to delivering reliable, ethical, and high-quality legal services. We are committed to upholding justice and providing practical legal solutions tailored to the needs of individuals, businesses, and organizations. With a strong focus on professionalism, integrity, and client satisfaction, our firm offers legal support that is both strategic and results-driven. We aim to simplify complex legal matters and guide our clients through every step of the legal process with confidence and clarity.

Overview of Our Services
    At KP & Associates, we provide a wide range of legal services designed to meet diverse client needs, including:
  • Corporate Law We assist businesses with contracts, compliance, company formation, and commercial disputes to ensure smooth and lawful operations.
  • Family Law Our firm handles sensitive family matters such as divorce, child custody, maintenance, and domestic disputes with care and confidentiality.
  • Property Law We provide legal support in property transfers, land disputes, lease agreements, and real estate transactions.
  • Contract Law We specialize in drafting, reviewing, and enforcing contracts to ensure our clients rights and obligations are clearly defined and legally protected. We also assist in resolving contract disputes and breaches.
  • Civil Litigation We represent clients in civil court cases, ensuring strong advocacy in disputes and legal proceedings
